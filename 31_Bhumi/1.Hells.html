1 Yojana = 16 kilometers

The lower hald consists of stone called Silāpaṭhavī 120000 yojanas thick

The earth is resting on a layer of ice that is 480000 yojanas thick

Beneath the ice layer is a layer of wind 960000 yojanas thick

After the wind is lower void, Heṭṭhima Ajatākāsa

After the lower void is the upper void Uparima Ajatākāsa
