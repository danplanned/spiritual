Quiz for the juniors



Answer these questions based on what you know

Feel free to work together, but please maintain a respectful noise level

  1. What are the triple gem? (There are 3 answers)
  2. For each of the triple gem, explain from your understanding what each of the triple gem are.
  3. What are the 10 ways to create merit? (Hint there are 10.)
  4. For each of the 10 ways to create merit, give an example for each.
  5. What are the 4 realms of suffering?
  6. For each of the realms of suffering, give an example of a being there.
  7. What are the 5 precepts? Write out each precept.
